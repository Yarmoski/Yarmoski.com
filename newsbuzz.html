News Buzz

Humans think in associations. This is why when given a list of related words, people will adamantly assert that similar words were on the list when in fact they were not. News Buzz takes adavantage of this thinking paradigm in humans and uses it to serve as a productivity booster and easily accessible archive for the world's top news.

News Buzz auto-posts the most relevant words in the news. The script is designed to generate a post once every day. The posts are automatically uploaded to the News Buzz Website.
