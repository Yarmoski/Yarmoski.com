Picky!

Check out the repo!

Picky utilizes the Yelp Fusion API to help you decide what to do. The picture isn't too relevant but I thought it looked interesting. Thanks website and enter in a location. Picky will provide you with one image at a time. If you want to learn more about the business that the image is from, simply click on the image and the Yelp page will open in a new tab. Click the "Another One!" button to advance to the next image.

In v2.0, you can now enter an optional search term. Example search terms include boba, lunch, scuba diving, backpacking, electronics, and bakery. Keep in mind that searches for uncommon terms will most likely yield fewer results. Without a search term, results will be of restaurants and food.
